# WanderSpot

## Overview

WanderSpot is a lightweight community-driven web platform that allows users to discover, rate, review, and share interesting places to visit around the world.

The goal of the project is to create a collaborative travel guide where people can contribute recommendations and help build a collection of unique destinations discovered through personal experiences. Unlike traditional travel platforms that focus on bookings and commercial services, WanderSpot focuses on discovering and evaluating places worth visiting.

As an MVP (Minimum Viable Product), the platform prioritizes simplicity and ease of use. Users can explore a catalog of places, search for destinations, submit new locations, and leave ratings and reviews without requiring account creation or authentication.

## Main Features

### Place Discovery

Users can browse a collection of places submitted by the community.

### Search and Filtering

Users can quickly find places using:

* Text search
* Category filters
* Country filters
* City filters

### Place Details

Each place includes:

* Title
* Description
* Category
* Country
* City
* Images
* Average rating
* Submission date

### Community Contributions

Users can add new places to the platform by providing basic information and images.

### Ratings

Visitors can rate places using a simple 1-to-5 star system. Ratings contribute to the overall score displayed on each place page.

### Reviews

Users can leave written reviews describing their experience, impressions, or recommendations related to a specific place.

### Image Gallery

Each place can contain one or more images to help visitors better understand the location and its attractions.

## Categories

The platform supports several categories, including:

* Museums
* Monuments
* Viewpoints
* Nature
* Beaches
* Restaurants
* Cafés
* Cultural Places
* Hidden Gems

## MVP Scope

The first version focuses on validating the core idea of community-generated place discovery and feedback.

To keep the project small and achievable, the MVP intentionally excludes:

* User authentication
* User profiles
* Favorites and wishlists
* Social features
* Booking integrations
* Advanced moderation systems
* Recommendation algorithms

## Project Goals

The primary objective of WanderSpot is to provide a simple and intuitive way to discover, evaluate, and share interesting places around the world.

The project focuses on:

* Community-generated content
* Place discovery
* Ratings and reviews
* Simple content management
* Search and filtering
* Clean and accessible user experience

WanderSpot is designed as a lightweight alternative to large travel platforms, offering a focused experience centered around discovering places worth visiting through community contributions and authentic reviews.
